# Attendance Tracking

Officers can mark attendance for event participants with three statuses: present, absent, or excused. Attendance records are stored both as top-level documents and as event subcollections for efficient querying.

- **Bulk Marking** — Mark attendance for multiple participants simultaneously
- **Status Options** — Present, Absent, Excused
- **Audit Trail** — Every attendance change is logged with who marked it and when
- **Member View** — Members can view their personal attendance history
